About Us

PiratePath is an android application that assists in navigating the entire Lyceum of the Philippines Cavite Campus. It is your ultimate tool for enhancing campus tours and navigating through the entirety of our campus grounds. Immerse yourself in interactive campus tours, where AR technology provides detailed insights into our facilities, landmarks, and points of interest.
